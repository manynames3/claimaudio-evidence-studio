Claimant: Yes, I was driving the Corolla.

Adjuster: Was that something you were doing as part of your job duties?
Claimant: I guess yes. I was doing it because my manager asked.
Adjuster: Tell me what happened just before the impact.
Claimant: I was in the middle lane on Fletcher Avenue. Traffic was moving, not stopped. The white SUV came up on my right and hit me.
Adjuster: Were you changing lanes at the time?

Adjuster: Had you started to move toward the right lane?

Adjuster: Did you activate your turn signal?
Claimant: I believe I did. I always use my signal.
Adjuster: Are you certain the signal was on?
